Efficient use and maintenance

Every two weeks the Purifaaya should be cleaned as follows:

  • Take a clean cloth and wipe the inside of the clay to clear out any dirt- do NOT use soap
  • Take the clay portion out of the plastic, and wash the plastic bucket, tap, and cover with water AND soap (just like a normal dish)

    • In addition to regular cleaning, water should be poured into the filter for continuous filtration and to ensure that there will always be enough safe water in the bottom of the bucket.

Efficient use and maintenance

  • Clean the filter body, diffuser plate and lid regularly
  • Flow rate should be about ½ liter per minute. In case the flow rate is very slow, carry out the “swirl and dump” exercise to reduce the amount of silt on top of the sand
  • Remove the lid and diffuser plate
  • Stir the uppermost ½ inch of sand. Do not mix the top of the sand deeper into the filter
  • Discard the turbid water but not remove the sand

Efficient use and maintenance

  • Use only biologically contaminated water, at recommended turbidity
  • Water should be consumed within three days of the SolarSack process
  • SolarSack should be regularly rinsed to prevent a buildup of soil and there sediments
  • Do not repair
  • Does not work in cloudy or rainy weather
  • Take care with sharp objects
